  • About Me

    Hello, My name is Joshua Findlay, I'm a fullstack developer with Resilient Coders. I'm the child of two Jamaican Immigrants who came here as a means of getting a better life for their children. From an early age I would watch my father fix every, and anything with his hands. He was a problem solver. He always taught me that when it came to skills, if you can do it with your hands not only can no one take that away from you but you will always be needed. I took this piece of information and ran with it.

    For me, I wasn't fixing physical things but I became a digital problem solver. I loved tech, I loved being around tech, and I loved learning ways to use technology as a tool to problem solve. Fast forward a few years and here I am, building and fixing things with my two hands, just like my dad. The main difference here is I don't have screwdrivers and hammers as my tools, I have a vast knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript and much more at my disposal. With all of these tools in my arsenal I can assure you that I can help you boost and polish your online presence.
